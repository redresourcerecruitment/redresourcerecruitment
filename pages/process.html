OUR PROCESS

Connecting You with Top Talent

1. CONSULTATION

We start with a one-on-one consultation with our expert recruiters to understand your specific needs and expectations.

2. MATCHING

Within five working days, we identify the ideal candidate from our extensive pool of skilled professionals. Each candidate undergoes a rigorous selection process to ensure qualifications and cultural alignment with your company.

3. CLIENT INTERVIEW

You have the opportunity to interview the selected candidate to assess their capabilities, communication skills, and compatibility with your business needs.

4. AGREEMENT

Once the candidate is selected, we finalize the necessary paperwork and agreements to ensure clarity and compliance. Our team ensures a smooth transition to onboarding, eliminating unnecessary delays.

5. ONBOARDING

We facilitate a seamless onboarding process. We organize initial sessions with your team to discuss the standard day-to-day activities and expectations. This includes a clear role and a detailed work document for the candidate. Our team takes responsibility for training the selected professional.

6. FOLLOW-UP

Our commitment doesn't end with onboarding. We conduct daily check-ins with our staff to monitor progress and productivity. Weekly discussions with you ensure systematic and address any concerns. Should additional training be required, it is facilitated by us at no extra cost.
